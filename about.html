Who Are We

Supportbasesolution is about four individuals who have a sincere passion for caring for individuals of the society as a whole, we have a passion to support people living with disabilities. we all have been in the industry for quite a while, about a decade and over. We started our company early 2022 and we are committed to a 24-hour program for people living with disabilities with complex and multiple needs across Melbourne. We are a registered provider

Vision

Supportbasesolution believes in connecting individuals and communities to live an inclusive society where the rights of people living with disabilities matters and accountable for in responsiveness of a fair goal and life

Mission

Supportbasesolution aims at exceptional care for all individuals living with disabilities, to support and give informed decision making with the approval of the individual living with disability, to always abide by a person

Value

Supportbasesolution wants a community nd society where everyone is treated equally with:

  • Respect
  • Values
  • Flexibility
  • Honesty
  • Integrity
